Your web browser settings are blocking cookies.

"Cookies" are small files that are placed on the hard drive of your computer when you visit various websites. RoboX uses cookies to provide services to you, and to help ensure the security of your account. Your web browser must accept cookies in order to access all of the features that RoboX has to offer. Here's how you can get your web browser to accept cookies:

  • Sign in again. If your browser asks you to accept cookies, choose "accept" or "continue."
  • Learn how to change your browser settings to accept cookies.
  • Check that your computer's clock and calendar settings are current.
  • Try updating your web browser to the latest version of Microsoft Internet Explorer or Mozilla Firefox.
